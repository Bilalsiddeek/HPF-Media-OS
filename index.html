Week 1–2 rule: Volume beats perfection. Call, get rejected, learn the objections, refine. The script improves through the calls — not before them. Do not wait until you feel "ready."
CALL FRAMEWORK
Opening
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from HPF Media. Did I catch you at a bad time?" [If yes] → "No worries — when's a better time?" → Book callback → Log it. [If no] → Move to pitch. Rules: Calm energy. Not salesy. Don't apologise for calling. If asked how you got their number: "I reached out through your business profile."
30-Second Pitch
"I work with agency owners and founders in Dubai to build their personal brand on Instagram — we handle strategy, scripting, and editing. Most founders we talk to are either not posting at all, or posting and getting zero traction. We get them to where their content is consistently generating inbound leads. Is that something you're dealing with at all?" Rules: End with a question. Name the pain, name the outcome. 30 seconds max.
Objections
"Not interested" → "Totally fair. Out of curiosity — is personal branding something you've tried before or just not a priority right now?" "Send me more info" → "Of course — quick question first: are you currently posting at all, or starting from scratch?" Qualify, then send. Follow up in 3 days. "I already have someone" → "That's great. Are you happy with the results, or still optimising it?" "How much does it cost?" → "Pricing varies based on what you need — that's exactly what the discovery call is for. 20 minutes, no obligation. Does [day] or [day] work?"
Booking the Call
"I'd love to set up a 20-minute call with Mo — he'll walk you through the process and we can see if there's a fit. I've got [Tuesday 10am] or [Thursday 2pm] — which works better?" Rules: — Always offer two specific times. Never "when works for you?" — Send the calendar invite immediately after confirmation. — 24-hour reminder via WhatsApp or email. — No-show: one follow-up call same day, one email next day. Then back to cold sequence.

CONTENT SOP

How content gets made at HPF Media. Brief to publish. No ambiguity.

PRODUCTION WORKFLOW
Step 1

Brief from Mo

Format, pillar, hook direction, key message, visual references. No brief = no work starts.

Step 2

First cut — 48h

First version within 48 hours of receiving brief. Done beats perfect. Include notes on creative choices.

Step 3

Mo reviews — 24h

One round of specific feedback: hook, pacing, captions, visuals. Specific notes only — "make it better" is not feedback.

Step 4

Revision — 24h

One revision round. If feedback is unclear, ask before revising. Don't guess.

Step 5

Final approval + publish

Mo approves. Caption + hashtags included. File uploaded to Drive with correct naming.

NON-NEGOTIABLES

These aren't guidelines. These are the rules of being on this team.

Faith & Values
No explicit music in any content under any circumstance
No content that objectifies or disrespects — ever
If you're unsure whether something aligns — ask Mo, don't guess
Approval Flow
Every piece of content approved by Mo before publishing
No client-facing communication without Mo's sign-off on messaging
Accountability
Sales rep logs every call outcome — no exceptions, no skipping days
Weekly check-in is mandatory — communicate in advance if blocked
Deadlines are real — escalate same day if blocked, not morning of
Communication
If you have a problem with something — say it directly, privately, to Mo
Feedback is specific — "this doesn't work" is not feedback
We are small — every person's effort matters disproportionately

WEEKLY AGENDA

Monday check-in template. 30 minutes. Every week. This is how Mo stays close to the work without micromanaging.

The rule: Monday meeting reviews last week's numbers, sets this week's targets, and surfaces blockers. It is not a creative session. Save ideas for a separate call. 30 minutes hard stop.
AGENDA TEMPLATE (30 min)
0:00 – 0:05

Numbers review

Sales rep: calls made, pickups, booked. Content creator: deliverables completed. Mo: pipeline update.

0:05 – 0:15

Blockers

What stopped you from hitting targets? Honest answers only. This is the most important part of the meeting.

0:15 – 0:25

This week's targets

Confirm numbers for each person. Write them down. These get reviewed next Monday.

0:25 – 0:30

Close

Each person says their single most important task for the week. Done.
